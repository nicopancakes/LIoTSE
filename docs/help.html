FAQ common questions about CamFIND
Q: Should I Use a VPN with this Running?
A: Yes. This is strongly recommended, This tool sends large amounts of connection attempts. Without a VPN, your ISP may flag your IP. In worse cases you could receive abuse complaints.
Q: Is this Legal?
A: Scanning public IPs is generally in a legal gray area. Accessing or viewing cameras without authorization may violate laws in many countries. This tool is provided for educational and research purposes only.
Q: This tool Seems Slow for Me.
A: Try Increasing THREADS, use the chart above for a guide. Results vary alot depending on your location and currnet network conditions.
Q: How do I Stop the Tool?
A: Press CTRL+C in the terminal on your keyboard.
